Online Alarm Clock

Free, online alarm clock since 2009 has been offering the best choice of application those who can not lose time, so as to wake up for reminders throughout the day.

The online clock, after being synchronized with the time of the user's PC, will display on your dashboard options for setting the alarm.

Heading level 2

Among its highlights is the fact that it is not necessary to download: users only have to access the website and set the alarm!

For work completly online, the alarm clock is compatible with any desktop PC or notebook computer (laptop), regardless of operating system is Windows, Linux or Mac OS.

Heading level 3

It's important that the machine has the Adobe Flash Player plugin to the operation of software.

Actualy we don't have an app for portable devices (such as mobile phones, smartphones or tablets). In the future we intend to develop major systems (Symbian, iOS, Android, Windows Mobile) to meet the main models of devices (iPhone, iPod, iPad, Galaxy, etc).
